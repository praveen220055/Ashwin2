Popular and safest tourist attractions

(based on data from 2023)

Ooty

Ooty is not only one of the most beautiful hill stations in India but also one of the most well-connected.

Sikkim

Don't miss the top attractions in Sikkim, from monasteries galore to giant Buddha statues, wildlife sanctuaries, and plenty more.

Varkala

Varkala Beach boasts striking red cliffs against serene waters, offering a unique coastal escape perched along its picturesque coastline.

Pondicherry

Puducherry is obviously on the list of to visit places in India, among all avid travellers, thanks to the hype.

Goa

East meets West in this sun-soaked state, where Indian culture intertwines with Portuguese influences left over from a 500-year occupation.

Rishikesh

Often dubbed as the Yoga Capital of the World and the hottest white river rafting site in India, Rishikesh offers an array of activities that engage spiritual and adventure seekers alike
